{ Who Is Whisky Oriented Development? }

We're people who work in the tech industry in Victoria, Canada. We organize periodic whisky tasting events that seat 30 people. In each tasting a mix of newcomers and regulars get a chance to sample some fine whisky, learn a little bit about it, and socialize. We're also a Slack channel where people hang out everyday.

{ Who Can Come? }

Anyone in tech who wants to have nerdy conversations and drink whisky! If you can sit in a chair and drink liquid then you're adequately prepared. Whisky is a good social lubricant and it's easy to make friends in the relaxed atmosphere.

{ How Can I Get In Touch? }

For distributor and vendor communications talk to Graham. For social media inquiries talk to Jamie. For questions about past or future tastings talk to Jeff.
